Privacy Policy

Here's exactly what happens with your data when you interact with this site. No legalese. No 47-page PDF. Just the actual truth.

What We Collect

Very little. Here's the complete list:

That's it. No behavioral tracking. No fingerprinting. No shadow profiles.

How We Collect It

Forms on this site submit to a Google Apps Script endpoint that stores the data in Google Sheets. It's simple infrastructure. There's no CRM, no marketing automation platform, no pixel soup running in the background.

Why We Collect It

  • To send you Agent Architect (the thing you signed up for)

What We Don't Do

This matters more than what we do:

  • We don't sell your data. To anyone. Ever.
  • We don't share your information with third parties
  • We don't use your data for advertising or retargeting
  • We don't track you across the web
  • We don't build ad profiles or audience segments from your information

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. No analytics cookies. No advertising cookies. Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN, which may set its own cookies — that's Google's domain, not ours. If that bothers you, a browser extension like uBlock Origin will handle it.
