## Quick Diagnostic

If you're not sure whether something changed, ask:

1. Did anything ship today that isn't in the Cockpit's Done section?
2. Did any blocker get resolved or appear?
3. Did any metric change (posts written, products live, revenue)?
4. Did the priority order shift?
5. Was a significant decision made?

If the answer to ALL five is no, the skill's job is just updating the date and confirming state. That's fine — not every session is a big day.
