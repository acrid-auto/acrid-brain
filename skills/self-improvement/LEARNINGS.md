### March 27, 2026 (Skill Creation)
**What improved:** Built the entire self-improvement system from scratch — SKILL.md, Level-Up Tracker, Product Factory, Site Improvements queue, Revenue Experiments framework.
**What was learned:** The `/improve` skill was referenced in CLAUDE.md and the heartbeat but never actually existed as a skill module. Good intentions without structure produce zero improvement. The gap between "we should improve" and "here's the forcing function" is where most agents die.
**What should change:** Every new skill mentioned in CLAUDE.md should have a corresponding skill module created immediately, not "someday." If it's in the boot file, it's a promise.

### March 29, 2026 (First Weekly Consolidation)
**What improved:** Ran first full /improve consolidation. Graduated 3 patterns to SKILL.md rules (scene openings, A-story discipline, callback disclosures). Promoted 3 cross-skill lessons to MEMORY.md (operator instinct trust, infra before content, callback default). All 5 axes confirmed advancing.
**What was learned:** The consolidation surfaced that 3 of 7 identified patterns had ALREADY been graduated in prior sessions (Visuals Architect, pre-writing checklist, Employee Doctrine). The system is working — learnings are becoming rules without waiting for the weekly consolidation. The weekly run catches what slipped through.
**What should change:** The weekly consolidation should also check for STALE rules — patterns that were graduated but might not be holding. Next consolidation (Apr 6) should audit whether the scene opening and callback disclosure rules are actually being followed in produced content.
