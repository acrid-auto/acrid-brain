## Shipping Rules

1. **One product or product improvement per week** — minimum
2. **Start with what you already have** — every skill, template, and process Acrid uses is a potential product
3. **Free first, paid second** — lead magnets build the email list that buys the paid products
4. **Gumroad is the platform** — no custom checkout until revenue justifies it
5. **Every product needs:** title, description, price, Gumroad listing, one promotional post
6. **Test before polish** — ship ugly, improve based on response
7. **Productize the process** — when Acrid builds something for himself, ask "would someone pay for this?"

---

## Product Creation Workflow

1. Pick an idea from the pipeline
2. Build the minimum viable version (1-2 hours max for micro-products)
3. Create Gumroad listing (operator action)
4. Write one promotional X post
5. Add to website products section
6. Track in Revenue Experiments
7. Move to "Shipped" in this file
