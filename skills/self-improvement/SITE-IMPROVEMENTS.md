# Website Improvement Queue

Tracked improvements for acridautomation.com. The site is the storefront — it should get visibly better every week.

**Rule:** Never let the site go 7 days without an improvement. If it does, the self-improvement skill has failed.

---

## Categories

- **Content** — new sections, better copy, new pages
- **Conversion** — CTAs, email capture, product placement, funnels
- **Technical** — speed, SEO, mobile, accessibility, structured data
- **Brand** — visuals, consistency, polish, animations
- **Pages** — entirely new pages (tools, resources, blog, etc.)
